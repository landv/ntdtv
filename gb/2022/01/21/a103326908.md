<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>组图：小松鼠“陶醉”嗅花香 模样超可爱</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522624-et-ground-2-web-36798-1-1-1200x720-800x450.jpg" />
<h6>荷兰摄影师迪克?范?杜伊恩早前到访奥地利维也纳郊外时，幸运捕捉到一系列大受欢迎的松鼠作品。（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人北京时间2022年01月24日讯】许多<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/野生动物.md#1">野生动物</a><ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/摄影师.md#1">摄影师</a>都会把握时机，在时序进入秋天之前，捕捉夏日生机盎然的画面，喜爱<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/自然风光.md#1">自然风光</a>的荷兰摄影师迪克?范?杜伊恩（Dick van Duijn）便是其中之一。</p>
<p>热衷拍摄<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/野生动物.md#1">野生动物</a>和自然风景的范?杜伊恩早前到访奥地利维也纳郊外的时候，幸运捕捉到一系列大受欢迎的松鼠作品。照片显示，一只可爱的<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/地松鼠.md#1">地松鼠</a>（Ground Squirrel）在维也纳乡村梦幻般的风景中“一脸陶醉地”嗅着<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/花香.md#1">花香</a>。这一场景的朴素之美令人着迷。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103329032" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329032" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522625-et-ground-23600798--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“从那天日出开始，我们就一直在拍摄那些<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/地松鼠.md#1">地松鼠</a>”，范?杜伊恩告诉《大纪元时报》，“到了第二天晚上，光线变得柔和宜人，这个美丽的场景突然呈现在面前。”</p>
<p>他表示，当时这只地松鼠不断靠近一朵黄色的雏菊，下一秒居然捧着花，将鼻子凑近想要闻<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/花香.md#1">花香</a>，而且闻着闻着还闭上了眼睛，就像人闻到香味时沉醉其中的反应，那模样实在太可爱了。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103329033" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329033" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522626-et-ground-23679-8--600x600.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="450" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103329034" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329034" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522627-et-ground-234576798-546354687--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>“我们注意到它们对花朵非常好奇，它们有时会闻一闻，有时甚至直接将其吃掉”，这位<ahref="https://github.com/yvrwxa344/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/摄影师.md#1">摄影师</a>补充道，“这一次，我很幸运地拍到了地松鼠闭上眼睛拥抱花朵的罕见一幕。”</p>
<p>范?杜伊恩还拍摄了其它一些松鼠作品，同样非常可爱。</p>
<figure id="attachment_103329035" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329035" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522629-et-ground-2367798--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103329036" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329036" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522630-et-ground-234576798-5463687--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103329037" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329037" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522631-et-ground-23677798--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_103329038" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329038" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522632-et-ground-234576798-54687--600x749.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="562" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>

<figure id="attachment_103329041" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img class="wp-image-103329041" src="https://i.ntdtv.com/assets/uploads/2022/01/id13522635-et-ground-236777998--600x750.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="563" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">（迪克?范?杜伊恩提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>37岁的荷兰人范?杜伊恩将野生动物摄影作为一种业余爱好，通过他的镜头，他还捕捉到雄伟的狮子、东北虎、猎豹、獾、猴子等动物的精彩画面。</p>
