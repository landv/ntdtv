<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>【禁闻】5.12祭奠 家长无自由权</h2>

<h6></h6>
<hr>
	<p>【新唐人2013年05月13日讯】<b>5.12祭奠 家长无自由权</b></p>
<p>5月12号是一年一度的母亲节，也是“<ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/四川.md#1">四川</a><ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/汶川.md#1">汶川</a><ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/地震.md#1">地震</a>”5周年。</p>
<p><ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/四川.md#1">四川</a>都江堰“聚源中学”在5年前的<ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/地震.md#1">地震</a>中，共有284名学生死亡，当天，亲眼目睹校舍在数秒钟内倒塌的学生家长周兴容向媒体表示，在孩子祭奠的日子，她也没有自由的权利。</p>
<p>因为前一天，有几十名国保及便衣人员围堵在周兴容家门外，告诉她“不要去学校” 。</p>
<p>而四川德阳绵竹市“富新二小”，20多名地震死难学生家长，在5月12号这天，因为国保大队警员和便衣阻止家长们放鞭炮，他们只能在校外烧纸钱祭奠孩子。</p>
<p>“富新二小”地震死难学生家长桑军，致电“中国天网人权事务中心”说，在此之前，当地官方多次登门提醒126名家长，不准前往震灾现场祭奠。桑军还表示，家长买的校方责任险被当地官员贪了。</p>
<p>在<ahref="https://github.com/blfjkf3657/ntdtv/blob/master/gb/汶川.md#1">汶川</a>地震中，约有7000座校舍倒塌，超过5300名学生遇难。不少学生家长要求当局彻查倒塌校舍是否涉及“豆腐渣”工程。但中共当局至今仍然没有承认，在汶川地震中倒塌的校舍存在质量问题。</p>
<p>2009年曾有专家调查地震灾区的44座校舍，发现其中57%损坏严重无法修复，比例是政府建筑物的4倍多。</p>
<p><b>山东费县500村民把村委会砸了</b></p>
<p>同样5.12这天，山东临沂市费县的村民们，捣毁了村委会。</p>
<p>据费县探沂镇石行村的一名村民代表告诉“六四天网”，探沂镇政府挖土卖钱，石行村村民12号晚间，再次前往镇指挥部抗议，几名妇女被镇委书记和黑社会打伤住院，政府不管。500村民把村委会砸了。</p>
<p>村民表示，被打伤的妇女伤势严重，仍在医院，因为当局不管，还没能打上针治疗。</p>
<p><b> 胡佳被国保殴打 禁外出</b></p>
<p>“六四”临近，北京维权人士胡佳被国保殴打，禁止他外出购买生活必需品。胡佳身体多处软组织挫伤。</p>
<p>据报导，胡佳表示，虽然他已经意识到随着“六四”临近，中共当局对他的监控日趋严紧，他已经做好精神准备，但是被殴打的遭遇，他仍感到有点意外。</p>
<p>胡佳说，“山雨欲来风满楼”，6.4对异议者的大监管即将到来。</p>
<p>编辑/周平</p>
<p>Parents Of Sichuan Earthquake Victims Forbidden To Mourn Their Children</p>
<p>May 12th is Motherds Day 2013, as well as the 5th anniversary<br />of Wenchuan earthquake. </p>
<p>In Juyuan high school of Dujiangyan, Sichuan, 284 students<br />lost their lives in the earthquake five years ago. <br />A parent of one of the victims, Zhou Xingrong witnessed the<br />school building collapsing in seconds when the earthquake struck.<br />Zhou told media that, even on the anniversary of the deaths,<br />she had no freedom to mourn her child.</p>
<p>The day before, many secret security police and<br />plain clothes officers blocked Zhou Xingrong&#8217;s door.<br />They warned her &#8220;don&#8217;t go to your child&#8217;s school".</p>
<p>In Mianzhu, many parents gathered at FuXin second preliminary<br />school on May 12th where their children died in the earthquake.<br />Since they were forbidden by security police to set off firecrackers,<br />the parents burned paper money outside the school to mourn.</p>
<p>One of the parents Sang Jun told 64tianwang.com, local<br />officials had repeatedly visited all 126 parents of victims.<br />They were warned not to hold activities on the spot.</p>
<p>Sang also revealed that, the school&#8217;s liability insurance <br />purchased by them had been embezzled by the local officials.</p>
<p>In Wenchuan earthquake, about 7000 school buildings<br />collapsed, resulting in deaths of over 5300 students.<br />Many parents required the authority to thoroughly investigate<br />whether jerry-built construction existed in those schools. <br />However, the Chinese Communist party (CCP) has not admitted<br />any quality issues in construction of collapsed school buildings.</p>
<p>In 2009, some experts examined 44 school buildings in<br />earthquake-stricken areas;<br />57% of them were damaged so badly that<br />they were unable to be repaired. <br />This is four times higher than that of government buildings.</p>
<p>500 Residents Smashed Building Of Village Committee In <br />Fei County, Shandong Province</p>
<p>On May 12th the residents of Fei county, Linyi city, Shandong<br />province smashed a building of the village committee.</p>
<p>The incident took place in Shixing village. A villager told<br />64tianwang.com that the town government was selling soil which was ravaging their lands.<br />When the villagers again protested at the town headquarters<br />on the evening of May 12, several females were assaulted.<br />They were injured by the town’s party secretary and <br />his gangster followers. <br />The angry villagers then smashed the building <br />of the village committee.</p>
<p>The villager said that the assaulted females were seriously <br />injured and were still in hospital.<br />The government did not take any responsibility for them<br />so they had not been given treatment.</p>
<p>Hu Jia Beaten By Secret Police And Grounded At Home</p>
<p>As the day of June 4th approaches, Beijing human right <br />activist Hu Jia was beaten by secret security police.<br />He received multiple soft tissue contusions on his body.<br />He was also forbidden to go out to purchase necessities.</p>
<p>According to a report, Hu Jia said that although he knew that</p>
<p>the Chinese Communist Party (CCP) was tightening control on<br />him as June 4th approached, he was mentally prepared for that.<br />However, he was still a little surprised at being assaulted.</p>
<p>Hu Jia said &#8220;coming events cast their shadows before them."</p>
<p>From his experience, a massive suppression <br />over June 4th dissidents will come soon.</p>
